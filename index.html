Business

Due to the success of these representatives, this emerging industry is increasingly being recognized by more companies. Every year, more and more streamers are signed and join the industry. By the end of 2023, the number of virtual streamers has significantly increased. From one streamer in 2016, to 300 virtual streamers in 2019, to over a thousand now, more and more streamers are joining this new track. Because audience time is limited, they have to be more excellent and unique to be favored by viewers. A variety of activities and fresh topics are constantly emerging.
